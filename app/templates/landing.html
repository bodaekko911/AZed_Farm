The Problem

When the business grows, manual operations start slowing the team down.

Sales are in one place, stock is in another, expenses are updated later, and managers keep asking the team for numbers. AZed ERP connects the daily work so everyone operates from the same system.

01

Problem: Stock is never fully clear

When stock is tracked manually, products run out without warning and sales teams keep asking what is available.

02

Problem: Sales and expenses are disconnected

Money comes in and goes out every day, but owners only understand the numbers after manual cleanup.

03

Problem: Too much work depends on messages

Managers lose time checking updates, confirming tasks, and asking who changed what.

The Solution

Each module solves a real operational problem.

AZed ERP is not just a dashboard. It is a connected system for the daily problems that slow down growing businesses.

📦
INV

If stock is always unclear

The Inventory module tracks product availability, stock movement, receiving, adjustments, and low-stock alerts so your team knows what can be sold before problems happen.

🧾
POS

If sales are hard to control

The POS module records retail sales, receipts, refunds, payment methods, cashier activity, and stock deduction in one workflow.

🤝
B2B

If wholesale clients owe money

The B2B module manages clients, invoices, payments, balances, outstanding amounts, and client performance so collections are easier to follow.

🏭
PRD

If production is messy

The Production module connects raw materials, finished goods, batches, losses, and output so production work becomes easier to track and review.

💳
FIN

If expenses are not visible

The Expenses and Accounting modules help record spending, payments, accounts, operating profit, and financial summaries without waiting for manual reports.

👥
TEAM

If everyone sees too much

User roles and permissions control who can access each part of the system, while audit history gives management better accountability.

How It Works

One workflow instead of separate files.

Daily actions connect automatically: products are received, stock changes, sales are recorded, payments are tracked, expenses are added, and reports become clearer.

  1. 01 Receive

    Add products, suppliers, stock, or farm intake.

  2. 02 Track

    Monitor inventory, production, and stock movement.

  3. 03 Sell

    Use POS or B2B invoices without losing stock accuracy.

  4. 04 Collect

    Track client payments, balances, and outstanding money.

  5. 05 Control

    Manage expenses, payroll, permissions, and audit history.

  6. 06 Decide

    Use reports to understand sales, stock, spending, and profit.
