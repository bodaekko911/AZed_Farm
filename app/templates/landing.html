The problem

Running a farm shouldn't mean fighting data chaos

Agricultural businesses carry enormous operational complexity. Most farms still rely on disconnected tools that create more problems than they solve.

Spreadsheet overload

Multiple disconnected spreadsheets for inventory, sales, HR, and farm output. Manual data entry leads to costly errors, version conflicts, and missing records.

No visibility into production

Farm batches, yields, and processing stages tracked informally. Managers can't see real-time status across multiple farms, causing poor decisions and harvest delays.

Fragmented financial tracking

Sales, expenses, payroll, and purchasing live in separate systems. Reconciling end-of-month finances takes days, and errors only surface when it's too late to fix.

HR & payroll pain

Tracking attendance for field workers assigned to different farms, calculating payroll with loans and deductions — done manually on paper, every single month.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

Questions from farm owners and managers before they get started.

Can I manage multiple farms from one account?
+
Yes — AZed ERP is built for multi-farm operations. Each farm has its own dashboard, production batches, worker assignments, and stock levels. You can view a consolidated overview or drill into any individual farm at any time.
Does accounting require a separate accountant to set up?
+
No. The system comes with a pre-configured chart of accounts suitable for agricultural businesses. Every sale, purchase, and payroll transaction automatically posts the correct journal entries — your accountant can review and export, but doesn't need to manually enter anything.
How does spoilage tracking work?
+
Field workers or supervisors log spoilage directly against a stock item and farm. The system records the quantity, reason, and date — automatically deducting from inventory and posting a spoilage expense to the ledger. You get weekly spoilage rate reports per product and farm.
Can B2B customers have different pricing?
+
Yes. Each B2B client can have a custom price list, discount percentage, credit limit, and payment terms. Invoices auto-populate with the correct prices based on the client — no manual lookups needed.
How is payroll calculated for field workers?
+
Workers are assigned to specific farms. Attendance is logged daily with automatic status rules (present, absent, late). At payroll time, the system calculates gross pay, applies any active loans or deductions, and generates a payroll summary — all in one click.
What is the carbon footprint module?
+
Each farm can log emission events — fuel use, chemical application, transport — against emission categories. The system tracks your total carbon output over time and compares it against reduction goals you set, giving you a sustainability report ready for stakeholders or certifications.
